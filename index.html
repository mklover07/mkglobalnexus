Public Awareness

Stay Aware. Stay Safe.

Education is the first line of defence against fraud. Know the common tactics criminals use.

📱
UPI Fraud

Scammers pose as bank officials or send fake payment requests. Never share OTP or UPI PIN.

💼
Job Scams

Fake job offers demanding registration fees or personal data. Verify every employer independently.

📈
Investment Fraud

Promises of guaranteed high returns are always red flags. Always check SEBI registration.

🏥
Medical Scams

Fake cures, unapproved treatments, and counterfeit medicines pose serious health risks.

🏛️
Govt. Impersonation

Criminals impersonate officials to extract money or data. Government never calls for payment.

❤️
Romance Scams

Online relationships that suddenly require money transfers. Verify identities before any transaction.
