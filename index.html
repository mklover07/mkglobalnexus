Public Awareness

Stay Aware. Stay Safe.

Education is the first line of defence against fraud.

📱
UPI Fraud

Never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone claiming to be a bank official.

💼
Job Scams

Fake job offers demanding registration fees. Verify every employer independently.

📈
Investment Fraud

Guaranteed high returns are always red flags. Always check SEBI registration.

🏥
Medical Scams

Fake cures, unapproved treatments, and counterfeit medicines pose serious risks.
